AtmosphereTest readme

Keyboard commands for GLCloud:
arraow keys       - turn camera
keypad 2, 4, 6, 8 - turn camera
keypad 7, 9       - roll camera
w, s, a, d        - thrust forward, reverse, left, and right
Ctrl              - hold down for 100x thrust
spacebar          - full stop
t                 - switch to show the optical depth texture
+/-               - Increase/decrease number of samples
F5/Shift+F5       - Increase/decrease the Rayleigh scattering constant Kr
F6/Shift+F6       - Increase/decrease the Mie scattering constant Km
F7/Shift+F7       - Increase/decrease the Mie phase assymetry constant g
F8/Shift+F8       - Increase/decrease the Sun's brightness ESun
F9/Shift+F9       - Increase/decrease the wavelength of the Red color channel
F10/Shift+F10     - Increase/decrease the wavelength of the Green color channel
F11/Shift+F11     - Increase/decrease the wavelength of the Blue color channel
